Are You Faster Than A Fifth Grader?

See how fast you can type against the average 5th grader. Too easy? Select other difficulty levels to see where you stack up. You can even go against the creator of this app.

Difficulty

  • 5th Grader (~25 WPM)
  • Students 13 - 27 yrs. old (~35 WPM)
  • Programmers 22 - 53 yrs. old (~55 WPM)
  • Creator < 30 yrs. old (~115 WPM)

Instructions

  • Choose your opponent on the left
  • Hit the start button when you're ready
  • The race begins when you start typing
  • Hit start to race again
  • Good luck!
